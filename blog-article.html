HOW TO BUILD A CULTURALLY RELEVANT IP FOR GLOBAL AUDIENCES

June 2025 • by Adewale S. Adekunle

In the creative world, Intellectual Property (IP) refers to original creative works of an individual such as films, TV series, books, characters, formats or even branded content that can be legally protected and monetized across platforms. A strong IP is a valuable asset that can be adapted, franchised and licensed globally. In today’s content-driven world, the stories that travel farthest are the ones deeply rooted in local culture. At Truss Media Group, we believe that building globally successful intellectual property (IP) starts by embracing authenticity. As a Nigerian media agency that is culturally grounded and globally ambitious, our mission is to help storytellers develop IPs that not only resonate in Nigeria but captivate audiences across continents. So how do you create stories that are proudly local but globally appealing? Let’s break it down.

Start with Home

The best global stories often start from the smallest, most personal places. Stories that stay true to their local culture, language and people tend to travel the farthest because they offer something unique. Audiences everywhere are hungry for something they haven’t seen before, something unqiue and honest. Trying to “internationalize” a story by stripping it of its local identity is a common mistake made by many Creatives instead of embracing the richness of your culture, the food, the music, the street slang, the fashion, which is what makes your IP stand out. The more specific you are the more universal your story becomes.

Find the Universal core

Every great story has a heartbeat. Love, family, betrayal, ambition, freedom, these themes are universal. They speak to people across continents and cultures. The key is to build stories that reflect African realities while tapping into emotions that anyone, anywhere can understand. This doesn't mean watering down your story but it is simply about finding its core truth that connects with the human experience.

Think Beyond One Platform

Today’s audiences aren’t limited to cinemas and TV screens. They are watching content on their phones, following stories across social media and engaging with characters in interactive ways. When developing a culturally relevant IP, it is important to think across platforms. Can your story live as a web series, a podcast, We are the collaborator who will respect your vision, see the global potential in your local story and who can help you reach the audiences your IP deserves.

Final Thoughts

The future of storytelling belongs to creators who are boldly themselves. Building a culturally relevant IP for global audiences starts with staying true to your roots while crafting stories that touch hearts everywhere. The opportunity to shape narratives, create unforgettable characters and build lasting global IP is here. The storytellers who embrace this moment, who build for now and beyond will lead the way. At Truss Media Group, we are dedicated to developing globally competitive intellectual properties rooted in local relevance and we are just a click away.
