About Daniel

Daniel Olaitan FM is the visionary founder and CEO of Truss Media Group, a leading force in cinematic storytelling and brand film production in Nigeria. With over a decade of experience, Daniel has directed, produced, and written for award-winning commercials, documentaries, and music videos, helping brands and artists bring their stories to life with creativity and impact.

Daniel's leadership blends creative direction with strategic insight, building a team of passionate filmmakers and delivering world-class content for clients across Africa and beyond. He is committed to innovation, excellence, and empowering the next generation of storytellers.
