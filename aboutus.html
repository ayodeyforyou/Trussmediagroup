Who We Are

Truss Media Group is a dynamic, full-service media and entertainment company based in Lagos, Nigeria. Founded by visionary storyteller Daniel Olas, we specialize in creating world-class content that bridges the gap between exceptional stories and visionary producers.

From idea to screen — and everything in between — we provide the structure and expertise to make every project seamless, elevated, and impactful.

To craft and deliver powerful, authentic stories that reflect diverse human experiences, fuel imagination, and transcend borders.
